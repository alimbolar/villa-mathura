Welcome To Bliss

A visit to Bali is not complete without a stay in Ubud. And with our rice field views, sunset point and traditional balinese hospitality, we invite you to experience it with us.

Our Family

Our family is actually the whole village as we believe in community living. However during your stay here, it would be me, Made, along with my wife Astiti and my daughter Chiara who would host you during your stay at our villa.

Our Rooms

Our villas provide you the best of both worlds. As they are traditional rooms with modern amenities, you can enjoy the traditional ambience and yet have the amenities that one is so used to these days.

Air Conditioning

Each of our rooms is equipped with air conditioners.

Wifi

A 75 MBPS wifi connection ensures you are never out of touch with the rest of the world.

Bathroom With Bathtub & Opensky Shower

You can choose to relax in the the bathtub or shower under the sky. All the amenties in the bathroom are replenished daily during your stay.

Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner

Traditional Balinese cuisine along with continental breakfast options are available all through the day.

Mini Bar

A well stocked mini-bar ensures that you are never out of your favourite drink.

Parking Space

We have ample parking space for your cars or scooters.
